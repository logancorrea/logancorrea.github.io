About Me

Hi, I’m Logan, a Biomedical AI and Data Science grad student at the University of Utah with a passion for uncovering meaningful insights from complex clinical data. My journey into biomedical informatics began with hands-on work in molecular biology and next-generation sequencing, and now I’m diving deep into machine learning to tackle challenges in healthcare.

Currently, I balance dual roles as a Biomedical Informatics Graduate Research Assistant and a Genomics Laboratory Technologist at ARUP Laboratories. In my research, I focus on analyzing large clinical datasets to uncover patterns and improve our understanding of diseases like obstructive sleep apnea. In the lab, I specialize in DNA library preparation, next-generation sequencing, and clinical genomics result verification. These roles allow me to bridge the gap between advanced data science techniques and practical applications in healthcare, driven by the goal of improving patient outcomes.

When I’m not working, I love immersing myself in Utah’s breathtaking landscapes. Whether I’m camping under a canopy of stars or hunting for unique rocks as part of my rockhounding hobby, the outdoors is where I recharge and find inspiration. At home, I enjoy cozy moments with my girlfriend and our two cats, indulging in life’s quieter joys. Curiosity drives me in everything I do—whether it’s exploring nature, unraveling a scientific mystery, or discovering something entirely new.
